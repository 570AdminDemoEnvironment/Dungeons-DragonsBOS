As an action, exhale a 5×30 ft line of acid. Each creature in the area makes a DEX save (DC 13) or takes 2d6 acid damage (half on success). Recharges on short/long rest.

⚔️ QUICK PLAY GUIDE — Your Turn in Combat
1. Move up to 30 ft
2. Action — Attack (warhammer in melee OR throw a javelin at range), Dash, Dodge
3. Bonus Action — Second Wind (heal 1d10+1, once per rest)

Key Tactic: Get to the front. Your AC 18 means enemies miss you often. Hit with the warhammer for 1d8+5 damage — that's massive at Level 1. Use javelins if a flying enemy stays out of reach.

IMPORTANT: Your warhammer does BLUDGEONING damage. In the Dwarven Excavation, ochre jellies SPLIT IN HALF if you hit them with a slashing weapon (sword). Your hammer avoids this problem!

Healing Potion: If the Cleric goes down, YOU have the backup heal. Drink it yourself or use your action to feed it to an unconscious ally.
