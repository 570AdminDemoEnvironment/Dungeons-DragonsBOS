Features & Abilities
Sneak Attack (1d6)
Once per turn, deal an extra 1d6 damage when you hit with a finesse/ranged weapon AND you have advantage on the attack OR an ally is within 5ft of the target.
Expertise
Double proficiency bonus on Stealth (+7) and Thieves' Tools (+7).
Darkvision (60 ft)
You can see in dim light as if bright, and darkness as dim light (grayscale only). No torch needed underground!
Fey Ancestry
Advantage on saves vs. being charmed. Magic can't put you to sleep.
Mask of the Wild
You can hide when lightly obscured by natural phenomena (rain, fog, foliage).

⚔️ QUICK PLAY GUIDE — Your Turn in Combat
1. Move up to 35 ft
2. Action — Attack (shortsword or shortbow), Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Hide
3. Bonus Action — (none at Level 1, but Thieves' Cant lets you leave hidden messages)

Key Tactic: If the Fighter or Cleric is standing next to your target, you get Sneak Attack (+1d6 extra damage) automatically. Try to attack the same enemy your allies are fighting!

Out of Combat: You're the scout. Check for traps (Investigation +1), pick locks (Thieves' Tools +7), sneak ahead (Stealth +7), spot hidden things (Perception +4).
