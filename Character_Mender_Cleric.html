Features & Abilities
Disciple of Life
Whenever you cast a healing spell, the target heals an extra 2 + spell level HP. (Already included in spell descriptions above!)
Lucky
When you roll a natural 1 on an attack, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll and must use the new result. Nat 1s don't sting as much!
Brave
Advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Your small stature belies a courageous heart.
Naturally Stealthy
You can hide behind a creature that is at least one size larger than you (Medium or bigger).

⚔️ QUICK PLAY GUIDE — Your Turn in Combat
1. Move up to 25 ft
2. Action — Cast a spell (Sacred Flame, Cure Wounds, Bless, Shield of Faith) OR Attack with mace
3. Bonus Action — Healing Word (heal an ally at range!)

Round 1 Plan: Cast Bless on all 3 of you (action). Then on future turns, use Sacred Flame (action) + Healing Word if needed (bonus action).

Key Rule: You only have 2 spell slots per long rest. Cantrips (Sacred Flame, Spare the Dying) are FREE and unlimited. Use slots for Healing Word to pick up downed allies, or Bless to buff the party.

If an ally drops to 0 HP: Healing Word (bonus action, 60ft range) gets them back up immediately. This is your most important job!

Concentration: You can only maintain ONE concentration spell at a time (Bless OR Shield of Faith, not both). If you take damage while concentrating, roll CON save (DC 10 or half damage) to keep it going.
